Spicy Hand-pulled Noodles

Description

A life-altering noodle dish that will make your mouth water just by thinking about it.

Ingredients

Noodles

  • Bread flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Sauce

  • Black vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Chili flakes
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Combine 2 parts flour, 1 part 1 water, and 1/100th part (aka a "pinch" of) salt in a big mixing bowl.
  2. Mix for about 5-10 minutes with chopsticks (or your hands) until a rough dough comes together.
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 more minutes until it becomes more smooth.
  4. Cover dough and let rest for 30 minutes.
  5. While dough is resting, combine black vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and chilli fakes into a small bowl for the noodle sauce. Proportions of each are entirely up to your taste.
  6. Chop up the green onion and add to the sauce.
  7. After dough has rested, knead again for 5 minutes.
  8. Check if dough has formed a "gluten window" by holding it up to the light and stretching it thin.
  9. Separate dough into 5 thick rectangles and cover in about 1 tbsp of your favorite cooking oil to prevent the dough from sticking.
  10. Cover dough and leave in fridge for at least 1 hour. Can also cover and put sauce in fridge.
  11. When it's lunch or dinner time, remove from fridge and strength each piece into a noodle using the "bang-bang" technique of slapping it against a flat surface and stretching until it's the desired thin-ness.
  12. Bring a pot of water with a pinch of salt to a boil.
  13. Add your stretched noodle and cook for literally 1-2 minutes.
  14. Serve cooked noodle in a bowl and top with the sauce made earlier.
  15. Top with more green onion and sesame seeds, if desired.
  16. Enjoy! The taste will live rent-free in your mind for the rest of your life.
