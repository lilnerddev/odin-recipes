Omurice

Description

A life-altering eggy-rice dish that is so cheap and easy to make, it will become your go-to staple.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Left-over rice
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Make the fried rice! Preheat a skillet with some cooking oil. Stir fry onion until fragrant.
  2. Add left-over rice, salt, soy sauce, and worcestershire sauce. Stir fry to desired done-ness.
  3. Pack rice into a bowl and then dump bowl onto a plate to form a nice mound.
  4. Now we make the egg! Add a generous dollop of butter to a new pan on medium heat.
  5. Once up to temp, add your eggs and stir gently.
  6. When egg is just starting to become firm, gently fold egg over to form omelette.
  7. Carefully slide omelette onto the mound of rice.
  8. Top egg with ketchup.
  9. Enjoy! Whoooooopie! Technically, if you did it right, the inside of the egg omelette should still be saucy.
