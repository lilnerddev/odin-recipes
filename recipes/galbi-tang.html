Galbi-tang

Description

A life-altering soup that will instantly cure the common cold, at least for the duration of your meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef short rib
  • Korean radish
  • Carrot [Optional]
  • Green onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Soak the short rib in cold water for 20 minutes to purify all the evil.
  2. Fill a large pot with new water and bring it to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, reduce heat to medium-low and add the short rib.
  4. Add the radish, carrot, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes, or until the veggies are tender.
  5. Remove the radish and carrot from the pot, and set aside. This is prevent over-cooking of the veg.
  6. Continue to simmer short rib for 3-6 hours, preferably until the meat just slides off the bones.
  7. Once meat is cooked to perfect tender-ness, turn off heat.
  8. While still pipping hot, add short rib and broth to your bowl.
  9. Slice the previously cooked radish and carrot into smaller pieces and add to the bowl.
  10. Top with chopped green onion and a sprig of parsley for aesthetics effect.
  11. Done! Consume to feel the soup warm your very soul.
